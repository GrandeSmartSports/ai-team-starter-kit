The timeline

Week 1 (Feb 12)
One agent, one terminal
Started using Claude Code. Built a simple softball evaluation tool. Realized this could be bigger.
Week 2
First pair: Builder + QA
Added a second agent for testing. Immediately hit memory and coordination problems. Started building solutions.
Week 3
Identity anchoring invented
Agents kept drifting from their roles. Created files on disk that agents re-read to stay on track. Problem solved permanently.
Week 4
Scaled to 4 agents
Added a Team Lead and a Designer. Built the communication system (inbox/outbox) to prevent chaos. Started shipping faster.
Week 5
Two sub-teams, 6 agents
Split into Team 1 (Build) and Team 2 (Brain). Each with their own lead. Parallel work without conflicts.
Week 6
8 agents, daily shipping
Added Forge and Patch as independent agents. Full team operational. Multiple products shipping in parallel.
Week 7 (now)
Packaged the system
Built the AI Team Starter Kit. Created the course. Launched AI Team Builders community. Teaching what I learned.
