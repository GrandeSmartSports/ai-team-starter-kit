# Skool Category Setup
## Create these categories in your Skool community

---

### Category 1: General
**Name:** General
**Description:** Main discussion. Questions, ideas, and conversation about AI agent teams.
**Notes:** This is Skool's default. Keep it.

### Category 2: Wins
**Name:** Wins
**Description:** Share what your AI team shipped this week. Screenshots, links, and bragging encouraged.
**Notes:** This drives engagement. Celebrate every member's progress.

### Category 3: Help / Q&A
**Name:** Help
**Description:** Stuck on something? Post here. The community and I will help you get unstuck.
**Notes:** Respond to every question. This is where trust is built.

### Category 4: Resources
**Name:** Resources
**Description:** Starter kit downloads, guides, templates, and scripts. Everything you need to build your AI team.
**Notes:** Pin the "Start Here" guide and the starter kit zip here.

### Category 5: Behind the Scenes
**Name:** Behind the Scenes
**Description:** Real posts from my daily work with 8 AI agents. What I built, what broke, and what I learned.
**Notes:** This is your weekly content. One post per week from real work.

---

## Setup Steps:
1. Go to your Skool community settings
2. Click "Categories"
3. Create each category above (General already exists)
4. Pin the Welcome post in General
5. Pin the Start Here guide in Resources
6. Upload the starter kit zip in Resources
